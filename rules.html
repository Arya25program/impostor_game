Instructions & Rules

  • Each player is given a secret role: either a Crewmate or an Impostor.
  • Crewmates must complete all their tasks to win.
  • Impostors must sabotage or deceive to eliminate Crewmates.
  • After each round, players discuss and vote to find the Impostor.
  • If all Crewmates finish tasks or the Impostor is caught — Crewmates win.
  • If the Impostor eliminates enough Crewmates — Impostor wins.
